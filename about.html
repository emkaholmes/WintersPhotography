About Me

Hello, and welcome to Winters Photography! Thank you for partaking in this journey with me. My name is Jacob Winters, and I'm a photographer based in Pleasant Garden, North Carolina. I aspire to produce images that capture the essence of human and natural beauty. An avid photographer for nearly 40 years, I specialize in: Fine Art Photography and Portraiture, Landscape Photography and Video, and Events and Projects. Let me create an image for you that will touch your heart for a lifetime.
